Why Redefine?

Firstly, we all know that any system improves itself over time with feedback. As we see it, there is a gap in the way feedback flows in conventional education. Simply put, how many of us who graduate out of the Indian education system, give back what we have learnt to the community?

Secondly, we also understood that most of the schools are under-equipped to enable a student with the right nudge, exposure and skill-sets. The students lack basic employability skills, let alone be creative.

We evaluated the current scenario to understand the gap in the system. It quickly became visible that itâ€™s a supply-demand problem. We have skilled artists and graduates on one side who want to pursue their passion after graduating. We have students who are in dire need of direction, and the nudge. We intend to bridge this gap, thereby redefining education and revolutionizing the way in which people learn, grow and teach.
